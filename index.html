About VasDhara

VasDhara Design Studio was founded to bring heartfelt design to everyday spaces. Named for the harmony between "Vas" (living) and "Dhara" (flow), we craft spaces that are functional, sustainable and beautiful. Our team includes architects, interior designers and project managers with experience across residential and commercial projects.
